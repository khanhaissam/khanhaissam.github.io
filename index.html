About

I started in engineering and manufacturing, moved into project management and PMO leadership, and now work at the intersection of operational excellence and emerging technology at Air Liquide Japan.

I lead digital transformation and continuous improvement initiatives — building the systems, standards, and tools that help project teams execute with more clarity and less friction. Not because AI is trendy, but because I keep finding real gaps where it delivers value.

I come from an operations background, which means every tool I build starts with a real problem, not a technology looking for a use case.

Good project management is about people first, tools second — I build the tools so the people can do their best work.
